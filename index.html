Touring an Ancient Bridge

The bridge was built in the 19th Century by the British colonial government. It's located in the interior parts of East Africa, Kenya. The bridge has proven to be one of the longest and largest bridges to be built in the year 1910. This is the time where Kenya was still uncivilized, underdeveloped and more so very primitive. With the help of our great grandfathers who were used to offer cheap labor, the London based Sir William Arrol & Co. LTD engineers built the bridge with ease.
